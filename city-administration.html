City Administration

Overview

The City Administration of Negele Borana is committed to providing essential public services, ensuring local development, and promoting the welfare of its residents. Located in the Guji Zone of the Oromia Region, the administration acts as the central hub for local governance, urban planning, and community initiatives.

Since its establishment in the early 20th century, the city has evolved into a key administrative center, overseeing diverse demographics and supporting local economic growth. The administration comprises various departments designed to address the needs of over 35,000 residents through structured governmental frameworks.
