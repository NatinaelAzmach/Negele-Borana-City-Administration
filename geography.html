Geography & Climate

Negele Borana experiences an altitude-moderated hot semi-arid climate (Köppen BSh), which is more akin to Kenya's climate than that of northern Ethiopia.

The town has two short wet seasons, occurring in April-May and October-November. These four months contribute significantly to the annual rainfall, accounting for 445 millimeters (17.52 inches) out of a total of 550 millimeters (21.65 inches).

Temperatures in Negele Borana are notably milder than typically found in hot semi-arid climates. Afternoons are consistently very warm, while mornings remain comfortable throughout the year. In fact, Negele Borana is just $1^\circ C$ ($1.8^\circ F$) away from being classified as a cool semi-arid climate (BSk).
