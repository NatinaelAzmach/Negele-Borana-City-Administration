Meet the Mayor

Mayor Malicha Dika

Serving the City of Negele Borana

Biography

Mayor Malicha Dika has dedicated his life to public service, with a distinguished career spanning over two decades in urban development and community leadership. Born and raised in Negele Borana, he understands the unique challenges and opportunities facing our city.

Prior to becoming Mayor, he served as the head of the City Planning Department, where he spearheaded several key infrastructure projects that improved the lives of thousands of residents. His vision for Negele Borana is centered on sustainable growth, economic prosperity, and inclusive community development.

He is a strong advocate for citizen participation and transparency in governance, believing that an engaged community is the cornerstone of a thriving city. Mayor Malicha Dika is committed to fostering a vibrant and welcoming environment for all who call Negele Borana home.

Key Initiatives

  • Modernizing Public Transportation Infrastructure
  • Promoting Local Businesses and Entrepreneurship
  • Expanding Green Spaces and Environmental Conservation Programs
  • Enhancing Public Safety and Community Policing
  • Investing in Education and Youth Development
