People & Demographics

The 2007 national census reported a total population for Negele Borana of 35,264, comprising 18,351 men and 16,913 women.

The religious demographic indicated that the majority of inhabitants are Muslim (54.89%), followed by Ethiopian Orthodox Christianity (34.25%), Protestantism (8.24%), and traditional beliefs (2.34%).

For comparison, the 1994 national census recorded the town's total population as 23,997, with 12,036 men and 11,961 women.
